Dual Avatar - Manifested A-Un
2 "Dual Avatar" monsters
This card gains the following effect(s), based on the original names of the materials used for its Fusion Summon. ●"Dual Avatar - Empowered Kon-Gyo": The ATK of your battling "Dual Avatar" monsters become 3000 during damage calculation only. ●"Dual Avatar - Empowered Mitsu-Jaku": Once per opponent's turn (Quick Effect): You can target 1 card on the field; banish it.
If this Fusion Summoned card is destroyed: You can Special Summon 1 "Dual Avatar Fists - Yuhi" and 1 "Dual Avatar Feet - Kokoku" from your Deck, and if you do, they cannot be destroyed by battle or card effects this turn.
